About Us  :

The premier student-led training and development committee at VIT Pune!

Founded in 2016, Abhivriddhi was established with the vision of empowering students to bridge the gap between campus life and the professional world. With a strong belief in holistic development, we aim to nurture individuals by equipping them with essential skills, fostering confidence, and inspiring self-discovery.

At Abhivriddhi,we meticulously design and execute events tailored to address the evolving needs of students. From workshops on effective communication and leadership to sessions on self-improvement and career readiness, every initiative is a step toward creating well-rounded individuals.

Through our collaborative and inclusive approach, we have created a platform that celebrates growth, innovation, and excellence. Abhivriddhi isn’t just a committee—it’s a movement dedicated to transforming potential into achievement and turning aspirations into reality.

Our Goals  :

Empower :

Help students identify and develop their academic and career interests.

Execute :

Achieve their short and long-term goals through expert counselling.

Excellence :

Enhance individual learning & development to make them industry-ready.
