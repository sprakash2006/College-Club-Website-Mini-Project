ABHIVRIDDHI

About Us  :

The premier student-led training and development committee at VIT Pune!

Founded in 2016, Abhivriddhi was established with the vision of empowering students to bridge the gap between campus life and the professional world. With a strong belief in holistic development, we aim to nurture individuals by equipping them with essential skills, fostering confidence, and inspiring self-discovery.

At Abhivriddhi,we meticulously design and execute events tailored to address the evolving needs of students. From workshops on effective communication and leadership to sessions on self-improvement and career readiness, every initiative is a step toward creating well-rounded individuals.

Through our collaborative and inclusive approach, we have created a platform that celebrates growth, innovation, and excellence. Abhivriddhi isn’t just a committee—it’s a movement dedicated to transforming potential into achievement and turning aspirations into reality.

Our Goals  :

Empower :

Help students identify and develop their academic and career interests.

Execute :

Achieve their short and long-term goals through expert counselling.

Excellence :

Flagship   Events  :

Mock GDPI

Mock Group Discussion and Personal Interview (GDPI) event. This event is designed to prepare students for real-world placement processes by providing simulated group discussions and one-on-one interview sessions with personalized feedback to enhance communication skills and proficiency.

To register for the Mock GDPI and other events at E.D.G.E, VIT students can log in to their VIERP accounts, navigate to the Events section, select "E.D.G.E," choose the appropriate category (with or without Mock GDPI), and proceed to payment. Non-VIT students can register through the provided link, sign up, select the event, and complete the payment process

Eaton Changing Gears

Eaton's "Changing Gears" is a collaborative program with Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT) in Pune, aimed at enhancing the employability of engineering students, particularly those from vernacular, rural, and economically disadvantaged backgrounds. The program focuses on developing soft skills, grooming, personality development, and introducing students to corporate culture.

Participating in "Changing Gears" helps students transition smoothly from academic life to the corporate world, equipping them with essential skills and exposure that complement their technical education.

Engineering Unplugged

Engineering Unplugged is an annual event organized by Abhivriddhi, the Student Training and Development Committee at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT) Pune. The event is designed to help first-year engineering students explore the essence of engineering beyond academics, focusing on personal growth, adventure, and self-discovery.

Participants engaged in interactive sessions, workshops, and hands-on projects, fostering a deeper understanding of engineering principles and their practical applications. The event also emphasized the importance of teamwork, creativity, and innovation in the engineering field.
